Why this number

Priced to land, not to negotiate.

This is what a 23-page B2B website with two distinct customer journeys, custom WordPress build, content migration, and a 30-day support tail actually costs to do well. It's competitive against Maryland/DC peer agencies and below the typical HubSpot-CMS bid for the same scope.

A.
Fixed bid, not hourly

You see the number. We hold the risk. No hourly creep, no "engineering time" line items at the end.

B.
Two revision rounds, named

Two formal rounds at wireframes and at design. A third round triggers a change order — predictable, never punitive.

C.
50 / 25 / 25 payment schedule

Half at signature aligns interests. Quarter at design approval gates the build. Quarter at launch protects you.

D.
Source files included

You get the Figma design files, theme code repository, and full credentials at handover. No hostage scenario.
