RFP §6.1 · GSA experience

Government & SLED — yes, we've worked on it.

The RFP notes that GSA or government contract vehicle experience is a plus. Two projects in our portfolio (Cap Energy, and a Maryland transit-services build not shown here) involved direct work for entities under GSA-aligned procurement. We understand the difference between a typical SMB buyer and a state procurement officer — different content, different proof requirements, different page weight.

For TSPEC specifically, the GSA contract (#GS-35F-0180M) and SLED positioning will be elevated in the redesign. The current site mentions GSA in passing; the new one will treat federal, state, and local government as a named market segment with its own page (per RFP §3.2: "Markets pages — Government & Education").
